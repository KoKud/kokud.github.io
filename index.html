My, Myself & I

I am a 24-year-old graduate with a degree in Engineering from the field of Computer Science, currently finalizing my Master's degree. With two years of professional experience as a Java Developer.
I am deeply passionate about new technologies. This enthusiasm drives my continuous development and exploration of emerging IT fields. Since discovering the Go language, it has become both my primary tool and my favorite programming language, often being my first choice for new projects.
I advocate for open-source software and maintain a commitment to writing clean, efficient code.
My technical interests extend into machine learning, neural networks, embedded systems, and microcontrollers. Beyond my professional life, I engage in creating intriguing projects, playing the trumpet, and enjoying films and TV series.
